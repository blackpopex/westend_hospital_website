The founding of the hospital

Westend Hospital was founded on January 4th 1978 by Dr J.E.O. Amadasun, Dr .Med., FA (ORL)., FWACS.,FMC (ORL)., FICS., MNES, Lifetime Member; Nigerian Medical Association (NMA).Almost 4 decades later Westend Hospital still remains at the forefront of healthcare delivery in Delta State and Nigeria thanks to our approach of continual improvement. Westend Hospital is the first private hospital to acquire a CT Scan machine in the defunct Bendel State. Over time we have continued in this kiazen tradition by investing in technology and professional capacity with our recent acquisition of a new GE CT Scanner and the setup of a Stroke Unit managed by our own in-house Consultant Neurologist.

The name “Westend” came from the fact that we wanted our hospital to be seen as representing the “western end” of orthodox medicine or rather, the level of medical practice experienced in Western Europe. orthodox medicine or rather, the level of medical practice experienced in Western Europe
