Biography

He hails from Umukpo-Okenne in Ohoba of Ohajil Egbema Local Government area of Imo State, where he obtained his early education and later moved to Owerri the state capital for his secondary school education.

On January, 1st 1981 he joined the Nigerian Police Force where he obtained his training at the police college Enugu, Enugu State formally Anambra State. The school is now known as Detective college Enugu, Enugu State.

After his basic training of six months, he was taken to the armory section, where he had a wide knowledge of the care, and maintenance of arms and ammunition.

He served in various arms of the Force, such as general duty, investigation, arms maintenance, chief clerk, exhibit keeper, crime recorder, prosecutor, and drill instructor, to mention but a few. Later he went to Ikeja Police College, Lagos where he was trained as a drill instructor and obtained a double certificate in a drill. On completion, he was posted to the Police Training School, Benin City. In the School, he lectured on Musketry, drill, and riot drill. He later made the Ok drill in the same school. He later moved to Shell Police commands Lagos, Port Harcourt, and Warri as the armorer Training and discipline where he retired from the force in 2009.

In 2015, the Nigeria Legion was formed and he was made the Ok Training. Later in 2016, he was appointed the commandant of the Nigerian Legion until 2019. In 2020 he was employed by the Deeper Life High School as the head of security until 2021. In his social life, he loves reading, writing music, and sport. He has written two books, Musketry Handbook with questions and answers and a Training manual for the Nigeria Legion Corps of commissionaires.

He is married to a beautiful, God-given wife, Mrs. Grace O. Ohiri with healthy children (four boys and two girls).

He is presently the Chief Security Officer (CSO) at Westend Hospital, Warri.
